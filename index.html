How It All Began

Our Story

01

The Beginning

Every great love story starts with a moment — a glance, a laugh, a heartbeat that says "this one is different." For Jei and Angie, that moment arrived quietly but completely, the way the best things always do.

What started as friendship grew into something deeper, something neither of them had planned but both of them chose — every single day.

02

Falling in Love

Through quiet afternoons and loud adventures, through inside jokes and shared silences — they found in each other a home that travels. A person who makes ordinary days feel extraordinary.

Love, for them, is not a single grand gesture. It is every small choice to show up, to stay, to be present.

03

The Proposal

When the moment was right — surrounded by the beauty they love and the life they had built together — Jei got down on one knee. And Angie said yes. A thousand times, yes.

And so a new chapter begins. One that will be written together, for the rest of their lives.
